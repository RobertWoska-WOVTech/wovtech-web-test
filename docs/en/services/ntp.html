NTP: time infrastructure

Accurate and consistent time is essential for security, auditability and incident response. NTP is a tier-0 service with broad cascading impact when it fails.

What we typically address

  • time source design (redundancy, trust, on-prem / cloud / hybrid)
  • synchronisation and drift monitoring
  • NTP hardening and safe client configuration
  • procedures for incidents related to incorrect time
