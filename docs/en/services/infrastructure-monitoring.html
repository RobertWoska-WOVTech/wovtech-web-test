Infrastructure monitoring (server/device)

We design monitoring for servers and devices so that alerts lead to action instead of fatigue. The focus is clarity, prioritisation and real operational usability.

What we typically address

  • server monitoring (CPU/RAM/disk, processes and services)
  • device monitoring (SNMP, availability, latency and basic health metrics)
  • alerting with reduced noise and escalation rules based on impact
  • dashboards for both engineering teams and management
